CEFTA-Transport Community Blue Lanes Have Been Established Between CEFTA and The EU To Facilitate Sea Traffic
 
“Blue Lanes” Memoranda signed between Albania, Montenegro and Italy on 9 September fully considers importance of customs duties and other taxes applicable during trade of goods, recognizes the need for cross border cooperation and takes into account objectives of the Common Regional Market Action Plan. The data will be exchanged electronically through #SEED+ system, speeding up customs procedures, formalities and controls.

CEFTA-Transport Community Green Corridors/Lanes Became Reality at The Crossing Points Between the CEFTA Parties and the EU: SEED System For Electronic Data Exchange Proves To Be Highly Flexible and Adaptable
 
CEFTA & Transport Community GreenCorridors/GreenLanes initiative has just become reality at the crossings between the CEFTA Parties and the EU. North Macedonia and Greece signed the MoU on the electronic data exchange, which is fully functional as of July 2021, thanks to the SEED+. Not only exporters from North Macedonia but all CEFTA Parties will enjoy the benefits, as SEED+ serves them all.

CEFTA 2022 Chairmanship Outcomes
 
MOLDOVA Chairmanship has set up ambitious agenda for 2022. More than 70 meetings of the CEFTA Structures were held, more than 500 experts were involved in more than 130 activities and prepared a number of the decisions aiming to further improve economic cooperation among us bring the tangible benefits to our companies.

 

Four important decisions await adoption by the Joint Committee: Decision on facilitation of electronic commerce, Decision on inter-Party parcel delivery services, Decision on prevention of unjustified geo-blocking and Decision on the recognition of AEOS Programs. All of them stimulate business according to EU principles and encourage investments.

 

Moreover, CEFTA is close to concluding the negotiations of another six additional agreements fulfilling the commitments the Western Balkans’ leaders made in 2020 – recognition of certificates, permits and licenses targeting engineers, travel agencies, tour operators, producers and traders of honey.
